A BRIEF ABOUT ME


Was born and grown up in a village under Sylhet district, Bangladesh. Completed Msc (Thesis) and BSc(Honours) degree in Forestry from the department of Forestry and Environmental Science, School of Agriculture and Mineral Sciences at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Sylhet, Bangladesh.

My research interest is on the Remote Sensing (RS) and Geographic Information System (GIS) in forestry, land-use, and climate change.

During my undergraduate university study, I was member of total four university campus organizations.I was President of Green Explore Society, a campus organization, on 2014-15 term. I administered this organization with the objectives of the nature and environmental conservation, promoting public awareness, conducting research and studies related to nature and environment. Likewise, I am a founding member of a student association in my university named as "Golap-Bandhan Student Association". I also worked there as Vice-President on 2014-15 term The association is formed by the students from Golapgonj Sub-District of Sylhet.

EDUCATION

Shahjalal University of Science and Technology | M.Sc. (Thesis) in Forestry

With distinction and CGPA 3.90/4.00 from the Department of Forestry and Environmental Science, Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Bangladesh. The thesis "Assessing spatio-temporal changes in forest structural attributes at Lawachara National Park, Bangladesh" was focused on the spatial and temporal changes of forest over 20 years under the supervision of Professor Romel Ahmed, Ph.D. The study on the forest cover change used two remote sensing approach; Disturbance Index (DI) and Forest Canopy Density (FCD) model. Click here to know more about the thesis.

Completed Bachelor of Science (Honours) with CGPA 3.52/4.00 from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Department of Forestry & Environmental Science.
