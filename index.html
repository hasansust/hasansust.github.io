A BRIEF ABOUT ME

I am a student just completed MSc in Forestry from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Bangladesh.

I have a broad research interest in the study of Remote Sensing and Geographic Information System (GIS) in forestry, land-use, and climate change. I was grown up in a village and from my childhood, I loved the nature. And that's why I enjoy my studies involving forest and other natural resources. On the other hand, I am always amazed by new technologies, therefore, remote sensing and GIS has drawn my attention for my study in the persuite of bachelor and master degree.

Meanwhile, I was in my university for BSc and MSc degree, I was also involved with two campus organizations. I was President of an organization, Green Explore Society, on 2014-15 term. I administered this organization with the objectives of the conservation of nature and environment, promoting public awareness, conducting research and studies on nature and environment. I was also a part of the executive committee as Vice-President of a student association in my iniversity named as Golap-Bandhan Student's Association which works for student welfare.

RESUME

EDUCATION

MSc (Thesis) in Forestry | Shahjalal University of Science and Technology

I have completed my MSc (Thesis) in forestry from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Bangladesh, with distinction. My thesis title was, "Assessing spatio-temporal changes in forest structural attributes at Lawachara National Park, Bangladesh" under the supervision of Professor Romel Ahmed, PhD. My thesis was focused on the spatial and temporal changes of forest over 20 years. I studied the forest cover change using two remote sensing approach; disturbance index (DI) and forest canopy density (FCD). To know more about me thesis click here.

BSc in Forestry | Shahjalal University of Science and Technology

I have completed my undergraduate study from the same university, department of Forestry & Environmental Science. My BSc academic research project title was, "Detecting tree richness hotspots using species area relationship to compare with land-covers and forest composition of Rema-Kalenga Wildlife Sanctuary, Habiganj, Bangladesh" under the supervision of Assistant Professor Sourav Das.

EXPERIENCE

Course Instructor / August 2017 - January 2018
Forestry Science and Technology Institute, Sylhet

I was a short term course insturctor on the Forestry Science and Technology Institute under the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change( website). I was Involved in the teaching for Forestry diploma courses. My responsibilities were to conducted classes and labs, set assignments and examination questions on Forest Silviculture, Geographic Information System, and Forest Inventory courses.

Research Intern / October 2015 - December 2015
Creative Conservation Alliance

As an intern, my task was to developed the distribution map of pythons in Bangladesh using GIS data. I created species distribution map of three diffrent species of pythons along with different forest types and water resources (river, lake, canal etc.).
